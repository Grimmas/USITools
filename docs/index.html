Welcome to Umbra Space Industries!

Umbra Space Industries publishes a wide variety of mods for Kerbal Space Program, including mods for colonization, construction, resource extraction and utilization, and various part packs. You can browse our offerings below. All mods are designed to work well together and the entire constellation can be downloaded in one pre-organized zip at the Github Repo. If you prefer to pick and choose, the links to each mod's current release, relevant KSP forum thread, and related Github repo are all below. Enjoy!

Want to keep up with the latest version? All USI mods use cybutek's Addon Version Checker (KSP-AVC) and check directly against the GitHub repo for any updates!

Like the mods and want to show your support (or just buy me a cup of coffee)? Click on the Patreon button below!
